Donnie Yen


Biography

Donnie Yen, also known as Yen Chi Tan, is a Hong Kong actor, martial artist, film director, producer, action choreogrpher, and multiple-time world wushu toumament champion..

Born: July 27, 1963
Age: 53
Education: Boston University
Spouse: Cissy Wang(m 2003), Zing-Ci Leung(m 1993-1995)
Children: Man-Zeok Yen, James Yen, Jasmine Yen
Awards: Hong Kong Film Award for Best action Choreography
