Martin Campbell


New Zealand TV director

Martin Campbell is a New Zealand TV and film director, best known for directing Bond movies GoldenEye and Casino Royale.He also directed Green Lantern.The Mask of Zorro and The Legend of Zorro .

Born: October 24, 1943
Age: 73
Spouse: Sol E Romero(m 2006), Valarie Trapp(m 2002-2006)
Upcoming Movie: The Foreigner
Children: Fabio Campbell, Tomas Campbell, Elina Campbell
Awards: British Academy Television Award for Best Drama Series
